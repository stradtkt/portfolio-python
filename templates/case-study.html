Case Study

A customer needed a website to run on a server. They were debating on what technologies to use. Since we have extreme capabilities with python and its backend frameworks, it woud be something to consider. They were also wondering if they should make it a full stack app, which could take more time to complete. The customer also needed a hosting environment. Depending on the amount of traffic flows through the site using technologies from the cloud such as AWS (Amazon Web Services) or GCP (Google Cloud Platform). Since those two are the best hosting providers i would suggest using them.

So what questions I shoud ask?

  • What problem do I need to solve?
  • Why am I trying to solve this problem?
  • Who will be the consumers?
  • When do you need to go live?
  • What is your budget?
  • Tech that you need to use within the project?
  • Are you going to run this on-premise or off-premis?
  • Where is the data coming from?
  • How will the data be collected?
  • Is there a need for data analysis>?
  • What is the teams familiarity with these technologies?
